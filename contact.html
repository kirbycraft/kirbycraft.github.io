Are you sure you want to contact Kirby?

I will try to reply to you if you reach out to me, but I can not always guarantee a response so please don't feel too bad if I stay quiet.

How to contact Kirby if you are a journalist/want to conduct an interview:

1. In your email request to me please use the subject line 'Requesting Interview [date]'.

2. Please provide a receipt and/or proof of a minimum amount of $100 of musical instruments/equipment/lessons you have donated to a school or religious institution in your area. This ensures at least something positive comes out of our interaction.

3. Please confirm somewhere in your email request that you are aware and agree to being recorded and documented in full on my website and/or website sub-pages including any and all of our conversations and communications.

How to contact Kirby to perform a live show

1. In your email request to me please use the subject line 'Live Show Request'

2. In the body of the email please include all relevant details, namely date and location but also include the time, if there will be other performances, if you have a specific song or style (electric or acoustic) you want me to play and finally how long the performance needs to be minimum and maximum.

Thanks for considering having me at your venue to entertain your patrons!
